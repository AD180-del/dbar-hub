GH-2016

Prof. Guo Huadong

Programme Founder & Chair

Affiliation

Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS)

Key Roles

DG of CBAS, Chief Scientist of CASEarth

Leading Chinese scientist in remote sensing and Digital Earth, renowned for initiating the Digital Belt and Road Programme (DBAR) in 2016. Academician of CAS and Honorary President of the International Society for Digital Earth (ISDE).

Specialises in radar remote sensing, Earth observation, and big data applications for UN SDGs. Author of over 500 papers and 24 books.

Radar Remote Sensing Digital Earth UN SDGs

CF-CAS

Prof. Chen Fang

Deputy Secretary-General

Affiliation

Aerospace Information Research Institute, CAS

Key Roles

Deputy DG of CBAS, Director of SDIM

Prominent researcher in disaster risk management and remote sensing. Serves as Chief Scientist for the DBAR platform under CASEarth and directs the CAS-TWAS Centre of Excellence on Space Technology for Disaster Mitigation (SDIM).

Expertise in GLOfs, landslides, wildfires, and droughts assessment across the Belt and Road regions using Big Earth Data.
