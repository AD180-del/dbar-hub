Institutional Persistence

The International Centres of Excellence (ICoEs) within the Digital Belt and Road (DBAR) programme represent long-term technical and institutional commitments between participating nations and the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

This curated dossier facilitates the integration of Earth Observation (EO) data into local and regional governance frameworks. By establishing standardised data sharing protocols and joint research agendas, the ICoE network serves as a durable infrastructure for global scientific collaboration.
