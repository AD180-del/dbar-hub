Institutional Persistence

The International Centres of Excellence (ICoEs) within the Digital Belt and Road (DBAR) programme represent long-term technical and institutional commitments between participating nations and the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

This dossier facilitates the integration of Earth Observation (EO) data into local and regional governance frameworks. While the **ICoE network** forms the core implementation layer, DBAR actively maintains **Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs)** with a broader range of strategic institutional partners—including the European Commission's JRC and the Group on Earth Observations—to ensure global interoperability of Big Earth Data.
