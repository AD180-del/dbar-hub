Decadal Evolution (2016 - 2026)

timeline title DBAR Decadal Evolution (2016 - 2026) 2016 : Launch of DBAR in Beijing : Inauguration of the "Scientific Silk Road" concept 2018 : Launch of European ICoEs : Expansion into Potenza (Italy) and Helsinki (Finland) 2021 : CBAS Establishment : Opening of the headquarters in Beijing : Launch of SDGSAT-1 (SDG-focused Satellite) 2023 : Implementation Phase II : Transition from data collection to decision-support applications 2026 : 10-Year Achievement Report : Full synthesis of Big Earth Data for the UN 2030 Agenda

Phase Analysis

Phase Duration Institutional Focus
Phase I: Conceptualisation 2016 - 2020 Establishing the ICoE network and data sharing protocols.
Phase II: Implementation 2021 - 2025 Launching dedicated infrastructure (CBAS, SDGSAT-1) and applying models to SDG targets.
Synthesis 2026+ Consolidating the "Scientific Silk Road" as a global standard for Earth observation.
