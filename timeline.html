DBAR Institutional Governance Timeline

Decadal Evolution and Phase Transitions

Decadal Evolution (2016 — 2026)

2016

Inauguration of DBAR

Formal launch of the Digital Belt and Road Programme in Beijing. Establishment of the "Scientific Silk Road" concept to leverage Big Earth Data for sustainable development across the Belt and Road region.

Governance Foundational
2018

European Network Expansion

Inauguration of key International Centres of Excellence in Europe, including ICoE-Potenza (Italy) and ICoE-Helsinki (Finland). Strategic shift towards polar and cultural heritage monitoring.

Scientific Internationalisation
2021

CBAS & Infrastructure Launch

Operational debut of the International Research Centre of Big Data for Sustainable Development Goals (CBAS). Successful launch of SDGSAT-1, the first satellite dedicated to the UN 2030 Agenda.

Governance Technical
2023

Implementation Phase II

Transition from fundamental data collection to integrated decision-support applications. Expansion of ICoE nodes in Africa (Sunyani, Ghana) and Central Asia.

Scientific Regionalisation
2026

Decadal Achievement Synthesis

Consolidation of 10 years of research outcomes into a global achievement report. Positioning the DBAR Knowledge Hub as a primary evidence base for the final push toward the 2030 Agenda.

Phase Analysis

Phase Duration Institutional Focus
Phase I: Conceptualisation 2016 - 2020 Establishing the ICoE network and data sharing protocols.
Phase II: Implementation 2021 - 2025 Launching dedicated infrastructure (CBAS, SDGSAT-1) and applying models to SDG targets.
